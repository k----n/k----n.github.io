Kalvin Eng

Ph.D. Candidate, Computing Science

University of Alberta

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computing Science at the University of Alberta, advised by Abram Hindle.

My research focuses on mining software repositories and developing tools for visual programming languages, part of which is being done in collaboration with Electronic Arts (EA).
